How to use Age Calculator?

Simply enter your date of birth and click on calculate. The calculator will display your age in years, months and days.

How is the age calculated?

Age is calculated by counting the number of years, months and days completed since birth. Leap years and months with 31 days are all factored in the calculations. So, you can expect a very accurate calculation of the age down to the number of days.
